About the hotel

A steady base
for the pilgrim city

Yamuna Inn has been welcoming travellers, pilgrims and families to Prayagraj for years — the kind of hotel where the staff remember your room number by the second day and the tea arrives before you ask twice.

We sit close enough to the ghats that early risers can walk to the confluence for sunrise, and far enough from the crowds that you can actually sleep. Every room is cleaned daily, every fixture works, and every rate is quoted straight — no surprise charges at checkout.
